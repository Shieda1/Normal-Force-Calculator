What is the normal force?
Normal force is the component of a contact force that perpendicular to the surface that an object contacts.
How to calculate normal force?
To calculate normal force you should use the following formula:
Fn = m * g * cosΘ
where:
Fn = normal force
m = mass
g = acceleration due to gravity
Θ = inclination agnle
Why use normal force?
The normal force is used to in order to keep a surface solid because without normal force an object would pass right through a surface.
